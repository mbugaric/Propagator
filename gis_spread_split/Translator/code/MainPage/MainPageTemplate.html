Instructions: You can customize all titles and inscriptions from English (ENG) to: Croatian (HRV), Italian (ITA), Slovenian (SLV), Greek (ELL), Albanian (SQI) and Montenegrin (SRP) cooded according to ISO 639-2 code. On the left side are English titles and inscriptions (that could not be changed) and on the right side are translations. Procedure is as follows: (a) Select the destination language. (b) Translate and fill boxes on the right side. (c) Click button ‘Save’ (located bottom – right) to save your translation. Character set is quite important. We suggest standard UTF-8 that covers almost all EU characters.
